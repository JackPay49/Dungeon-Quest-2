The Wand of Dying Stars
A staff topped with a crimson orb. This staff's orb swirls with the soul of a dying star and it burns to touch. You can barely stand the heat radiating off of this wand. 
When used this Relic will blast out a fireball infront of you that will travel until it reaches an enemy or wall. It deals 2 damage. You cannot use this relic if an enemy or wall is right infront of you!
