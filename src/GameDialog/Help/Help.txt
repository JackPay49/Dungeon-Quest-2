Plain
Dungeon Quest 2 is a level clearing game. Each level with have a random assortment of enemies on it. Every 10 levels you will come to a boss level where you can earn a Relic. Relics are items that use energy to perform some action. Each level can eitehr be an enemy clearing level, key level or chest level. In the first you must kill all enemies on the level to proceed. In the second you must collect all the keys and unlock the trapdoor to proceed. In the third you can proceed or open a chest by collecting a key on that level. If you die on a level you will revert back to your previous save. Some enemies will randomly drop potions to restore your health or energy. Energy is also restored by successfully hitting enemies with a normal attack.
